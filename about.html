Technology

SRX-T615 4K Digital Projector for Industrial, Visualization and Simulation from Sony offers 4K resolution, great brightness, color precision, uniformity, and contrast, creating it to be multi-tasking for several applications.
The 18,000 lumens of brightness displays ultra HD images with a native resolution of 4096 x 2160 pixels. The 12,000:1 contrast ratio creates bright images with a large dynamic range.
The unique benefits that are entailed: offers brightness using 4K optical engine, tilted installation, distortion correction for 4K3D, edge-blending for multi-projection, efficient and easy to handle, HPM multi-lamp permits simple replacement, interleaved lamp control provides considerable durability.

Abr Cinemas: Protocols under the Pandemic

Complying with the imperative WHO Measures under the new normal, Abr Cinemas has adopted the exercises viz. maintaining social distancing inside the entire premises including restrooms, contactless or paperless, risk-free transactions with the help of new normal technologies, making the wearing of masks a must, fully sanitized ambience at every section, usage of anti-microbial film, sanitizing high-touch areas at regular intervals, and disinfecting the auditorium before the start of every show for secured screening every time.
Food can be ordered using QR Code, UV cabinets keep the food and packaging disinfected before serving. The ticket counters will find cashiers wearing safety shields, usage of digital billings where one can check bill and balance.
