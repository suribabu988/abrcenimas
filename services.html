retailing

BOSS Group always believes in great customer service and quality products which results in success.

The ‘once upon a time’ small service station has now evolved to 16 convenient stores for all the Australians, under varied brand names that consist of freehold and leasehold under BOSS Group. For instance, BP, 7-Eleven and Caltex.

The primary aim is to acquire new customers, retain earlier customers and extend adequate loyalty to these customers. BOSS Group offers pure quality fuel with customer-convenience experience at affordable prices for the common man on the move, with utmost customer service excellence.

Very strong and important policies go behind the scenes of retail business here. BOSS Group strategies the relevant support systems that will continually keep the business alive and vibrant.

The main retail principle is to respect and stay focused on the customers, since the customers form the hub of the retail business, and everything performed, revolves around the customer segment.

This perspective helps BOSS Group to grow consistently.
