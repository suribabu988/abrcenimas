About Us

Over a decade now, our team has been working with various Indian restaurants in United States trying to tap the right flavor notes and experimenting varied Indian dishes. What we realized over these years is that very few establishments serve authentic flavors, while the rest choose to serve the conventional narrow variety.

Bona fide Indian food is time and labor intensive and the food is cooked with a lot of love and passion. Each region in India, every state, every home and it’s kitchen has their own story to share. But several regional food varieties don’t find place on the mainstream menu and several ethnic recipes and the stories behind them are ignored.
