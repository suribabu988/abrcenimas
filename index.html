Our Story

Emerging way back in October 1997, BOSS Group is now flaunting success in the age of information. With the passage of time, the company has steadily developed and diversified in multi-business corridors: retail petroleum, construction, renovation and operations of fuel stations, hotels and café, across Australia.

For instance, ever since 1997, the company has evolved from one 7 eleven retail store to 15 retail stores employing over hundreds of employees generating revenue over 75 million. Yes, that’s the flavor of success we’re talking about. Indeed, BOSS Group is future ready.

Our Mission

To perennially deal with all Australians with immense respect and care. Listen intently to their specific needs and excel in customizing them. Empower the brand by meeting customers’ needs in smart ways to keep delighting them always.

To continuously encourage the talent team to remain motivated and keep working with integrity and commitment. To pursue growth steadily and consistently with diversified revenue streams.

Diligently invest in our expertise, deliver innovative, inspiring and intelligent products, and relentlessly serve the target audience to escalate customer-performance.

Our Vision

Incessantly offer high standards of customer experience and endeavor to excel in all the realms of performances. While, with the passage of time, emerge as one of the largest independent fuel retailer, creating a brighter tomorrow for our stakeholders.

Envisaged to emerge as one of the most eminent global brands, and incessantly serve people of varied culture groups with their utmost satisfaction.
